# Writing style

- Do not use em dashes (`—`) in code comments, documentation, commit messages, or any prose you write. Humans rarely use them, and they read as AI-generated. Use a hyphen with surrounding spaces, a comma, parentheses, or a colon instead, or split into two sentences.
- Avoid the en dash (`–`) and the unicode ellipsis (`…`) for the same reason; write `-` and `...`.
